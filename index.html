  • What is a Chem Caddy?

    The Chem Caddy is the world’s easiest way to spray many solutions from ready to use 1 gal marked containers. When finished, simply flush out the system with tap water and cap your solution container and save until a later date. With additional hose spraying from tanks or 5 gal pails is another simple option.
